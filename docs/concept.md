# Concept & Pitch

## The Problem

Local businesses (bars, restaurants, cafes) lose revenue during major events because:

1. **No awareness** — Don't know which events are happening or when
2. **No promotion strategy** — Don't know how to create event-specific deals
3. **Poor demand forecasting** — Either over-order (waste) or under-order (stockout)
4. **Staffing chaos** — Scrambling to find extra staff last-minute
5. **Missed marketing window** — Posting promos after the event already started

**Real-world example:**
A sports bar in Jakarta doesn't realize Argentina vs Brazil is tomorrow. They staff normally, order regular inventory. 200 fans show up, they run out of beer at halftime, lose $3,000 in potential sales.

## The Solution: EventBoost

**"Your business + any event = more sales."**

EventBoost is an AI-powered platform that automatically integrates events into a local business's strategy. The business owner doesn't need to research events, create promotions, or predict demand — the AI agent does it all.

### Core Value Proposition

1. **Automatic event detection** — Agent finds relevant events (World Cup, concerts, festivals)
2. **One-click promotion** — Generates QR code + promo deal instantly
3. **Demand prediction** — AI predicts how many customers will show up
4. **Inventory management** — Recommends what to order and how much
5. **Staff scheduling** — Suggests how many staff to schedule
6. **Marketing automation** — Creates and schedules social media posts
7. **ROI tracking** — Shows exactly how much revenue the event generated

### Target Users

**Primary:** Local bar/restaurant owners near event venues
**Secondary:** Cafe owners, food trucks, retail stores near stadiums/concert halls
**Tertiary:** Event organizers who want to partner with local businesses

### Business Model (Post-Hackathon)

- Freemium: Free for 1 event/month, paid for unlimited
- Commission: Small % of event-driven revenue
- SaaS: Monthly subscription for advanced features

### EventBoost Differentiation

- **Automated** — No manual research needed
- **Event-specific** — Promos tied to specific events
- **Predictive** — AI forecasts demand
- **Actionable** — Agent takes actions, not just suggestions
- **Measurable** — ROI tracking per event
