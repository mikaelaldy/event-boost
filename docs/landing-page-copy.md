## FAQ Section

### Q: Do I need technical skills to use EventBoost?
No. If you can tap a button, you can use EventBoost. The AI handles everything.

### Q: What events does EventBoost support?
World Cup matches, concerts, festivals, local events, and more. If it draws a crowd, EventBoost can help you profit from it.

### Q: How does the QR code work?
Customers scan the QR code with their phone camera. They see your promo, enter their info, pre-order a combo, and pay. No app download required.

### Q: What if the prediction is wrong?
EventBoost learns from every event. If it overestimates, it adjusts for next time. After 10 events, prediction accuracy typically reaches 90%+.

### Q: Can I customize the promotion?
Yes. You can adjust discount percentages, combo items, promo copy, and terms. Or let the AI generate it for you.

### Q: Is my customer data safe?
Yes. Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share customer information.
